In the song “Gimme Shelter” by the Rolling Stones, the term “It’s just a shot away” is said a number of times. Count this number, and then subtract 8. This is the first number to one of the locks.

In the song “Before They Make Me Run” by the Rolling Stones, Keith Richards says the word “run” a number of times. Take this number, multiply it by four, and that is the second number on the same lock.

In the song “Jumpin’ Jack Flash” by the Rolling Stones, the term “jumpin’ jack flash” is said a number of times, this amount is the third number on the same lock.
